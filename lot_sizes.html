Method & caveats
  • Source: King County parcel_address_area feature service (lot square footage + parcel geometry), pulled 2026-05-21.
  • Which lots: parcels whose primary address is on the segment — Stone Way N #3400–4499 (N 34th→N 45th); 45th St between Stone Way and I-5 (same boundaries as the units analysis). One row per parcel; records with zero lot area excluded.
  • Median vs mean: both streets have a few very large parcels (e.g. 1815 N 45th at 75,112 sqft; 4400 Stone Way at 60,088), so the median is the better “typical lot” figure. Stone Way leads on both (median 1.7×, mean 1.4×).
  • Frontage & depth are each parcel's bounding-box dimensions along/perpendicular to the street (from geometry in State Plane feet); they slightly overstate irregular lots.
  • Orientation: both streets are drawn horizontally for comparison. Stone Way actually runs north–south; 45th runs east–west.
